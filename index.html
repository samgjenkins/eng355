About The Project

The Lage Wernstedt photographs are a collection of pictures and glass plate negatives taken between 1916 and 1935 by surveyor and cartographer Lage Wernstedt during his work for the United States Forest Service.

Using the AI tool NightCafé, a selection of these images have been recreated to illustrate the current potential fallacies of modern archival studies.
